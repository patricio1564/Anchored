## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I track my reading progress?**
Your progress is saved automatically as you complete lessons and quizzes. Visit the Profile tab to see your streak, XP, and level.

**How do I highlight and save verses?**
While reading a Bible passage, tap any verse to select it. Use the "Highlight & Save" button to choose a color and save it to your Journal.

**Can I change the Bible translation?**
Yes. When reading a passage, use the translation picker at the top to switch between WEB, KJV, ASV, BBE, and Darby translations. Some translations require a premium subscription.

**How do streaks work?**
Open the app and complete any activity each day to maintain your streak. Your current streak and longest streak are shown on the Profile tab.

**Is my data backed up?**
Your progress is stored locally on your device. We recommend keeping regular device backups through iCloud or your computer.
